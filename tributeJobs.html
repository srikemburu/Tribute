Steve Jobs

Steven P. Jobs, the visionary co-founder of Apple who Redefined Digital Age

Here's a time line of Steve Job's life:

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco, California
  • 1976 - Jobs and Wozniak co-founded Apple in 1976 to sell Wozniak's Apple I personal computer.
  • 1984 - Jobs introduced the Macintosh computer on January 24, 1984
  • 1998 - Apple iMac G3 was introduced in 1998
  • 2001 - The first generation of iPod was released October 23, 2001
  • 2007 - The first iPhone was released on June 29, 2007
  • 2011 - Dies at the age of 56.

"Perhaps it is a comment on the daily roar of opinion and the ugly rush to judge, but now, above all else, I miss his singular and beautiful clarity. Beyond his ideas and vision, I miss his insight that brought order to chaos.

-- Jony Ive, Jobs' friend and longtime design collaborator
